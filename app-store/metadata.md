## Description (4000 chars max)
Investigator Events is the global calendar and professional network for private investigators, corporate investigators, and intelligence professionals.

Browse confirmed conferences, AGMs, training events, and association meetings from organisations across the world — including WAD, ABI, IKD, FALI, CII, SPI, NALI, and dozens more.

KEY FEATURES:

- Global Event Calendar — browse upcoming PI conferences, training, and association events filtered by region, month, and organiser
- Professional Directory — find and connect with investigators worldwide
- Event Reviews — read and leave anonymous reviews of conferences you've attended
- Community Forum — share insights, post referrals, and discuss with fellow professionals
- Association Network — explore 40+ investigator associations and their events
- Weekly Briefing — get a curated newsletter every Monday with new events and community updates
- Push Notifications — never miss when a connection requests to connect or when your event submission is approved
- Profile & Networking — create your professional profile, connect with peers, and build your network

FREE FOREVER for individual investigators. No subscription required.

Built in partnership with industry professionals for the global PI community.

## Review Notes (for Apple reviewer)
This app serves the professional private investigator community worldwide. It provides a calendar of confirmed industry events, a professional networking directory, and community features.

The app uses a native webview connected to our live platform at investigatorevents.com, enhanced with native capabilities including push notifications, haptic feedback, native share sheet, offline detection, and biometric-ready authentication.

Test account: Please browse as a guest — all event listings and association pages are publicly accessible without an account. To test authenticated features (profile, reviews, forum), you can create a free account via email or use Sign in with Apple.
